# Read Me First: How to Choose Your Path

I have generated 5 distinct PRD (Product Requirements Document) options for you and your developer. All of them use the foundational idea of automated preventative screening for unattached patients, but they tackle the problem from different angles depending on what you want to emphasize to the judges.

### The Options at a Glance:

1. **[01-Option-1-ScreenBC.md](./01-Option-1-ScreenBC.md) (The Core Vision)**
   * *Vibe:* Official, government-backed, extension of Health Gateway.
   * *Focus:* Realistic patient portal, automated email triggers, AI interpretation.
   * *Best for:* Showing a highly feasible, immediate plug-in to the BC health system.

2. **[02-Option-2-CareCatch.md](./02-Option-2-CareCatch.md) (The Virtual Clinic)**
   * *Vibe:* B2B Health Tech, Population Health Command Center.
   * *Focus:* The physician dashboard, bulk patient processing, AI triage queue.
   * *Best for:* Proving scalability. Shows how AI can multiply one doctor's capacity to manage thousands of unattached patients.

3. **[03-Option-3-CommunityScreen.md](./03-Option-3-CommunityScreen.md) (The Pharmacy Model)**
   * *Vibe:* Pragmatic, physical-world integration.
   * *Focus:* Pharmacist UI, Point-of-Sale alerts, printed requisitions.
   * *Best for:* Impressing judges with health-system knowledge by leveraging the recent real-world expansion of pharmacist scope in BC.

4. **[04-Option-4-HealthText.md](./04-Option-4-HealthText.md) (The SMS Native App)**
   * *Vibe:* Ultra-modern, low-friction, AI-native.
   * *Focus:* Conversational AI, zero UI to build (just a chat window), instant access.
   * *Best for:* A "wow" factor demo. Chat interfaces that actually execute workflows (like generating PDFs) look incredibly impressive on stage.

5. **[05-Option-5-ProactiveHealth.md](./05-Option-5-ProactiveHealth.md) (The Consumer Health Score)**
   * *Vibe:* Silicon Valley startup, gamified, preventative.
   * *Focus:* Beautiful UI, health scoring algorithms, data visualization.
   * *Best for:* Standing out visually. If you have a developer who is great at UI/animations, this will look the most polished.

### Recommendation for Tomorrow Morning:
Read through them quickly with your developer. Decide if you want to build a **Patient Portal** (Option 1/5), a **Doctor Dashboard** (Option 2), a **Pharmacy Tool** (Option 3), or a **Chatbot** (Option 4). 

Once you pick one, the PRD provides exactly what your developer needs to start building the frontend and mocking the data layer immediately. Good luck! Let's win this thing.
