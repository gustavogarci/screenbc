To get real CIHI wait times data (Excel format):

1. Go to: https://www.cihi.ca/en/topics/access-and-wait-times/data-tables
2. Select the procedure of interest (e.g., Hip Replacement, Knee Replacement)
3. Choose the data table format (XLSX)
4. Download and save to this directory

Available tables include:
  - Wait Times for Hip and Knee Replacements
  - Wait Times for Cataract Surgery
  - Wait Times for Radiation Therapy
  - Wait Times for Hip Fracture Repair
  - Wait Times for Diagnostic Imaging (CT, MRI)
  - Wait Times for Cardiac Bypass Surgery

Note: CIHI data requires free registration. The mock data in
wait_times_mock.csv mirrors the structure and realistic trends.
